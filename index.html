Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 2026
Overview

Luminary is a local-first application. We do not operate servers, collect user data, or track usage in any form. Everything stays on your device.

Data we do not collect

We do not collect your name, email, journal entries, birth information, location, device identifiers, usage patterns, or any other personal information.

Data stored on your device

Luminary stores the following in your browser's localStorage: your profile information, journal entries, custom calendar events, AI memory context, app preferences, and API keys. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to export it.

API keys and AI providers

When you add an API key, Luminary sends your prompts directly from your device to the AI provider you selected (Groq, Anthropic, OpenAI, Gemini, or xAI). We never see, store, or transmit your API keys. Each provider has their own privacy policy governing how they handle your prompts.

Third-party AI providers

Groq: groq.com/privacy-policy

Anthropic: anthropic.com/privacy

OpenAI: openai.com/privacy

Google Gemini: policies.google.com/privacy

xAI: x.ai/privacy

Fonts and external resources

Luminary loads fonts from Google Fonts on first use. Google may collect standard request data (IP address, browser type) per their privacy policy. No other external resources are loaded.

Children's privacy

Luminary is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. You can export, modify, or delete all data at any time from the Settings screen. Clearing your browser data will remove all Luminary data permanently.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the date above. Continued use of Luminary constitutes acceptance of the current policy.
